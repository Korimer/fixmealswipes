What are Meal Swipes?

Simply put, a representation of a pre-paid meal. CSU provides several meal plans, each offering a number of meals per week. Meal swipes, then, are representations of how many meals you have remaining. One meal is roughly valued at $10 - that's how much purchasing power it has at any market location. It is worth noting, however, that you can use a single swipe to get into any all-you-may-eat location on campus - something that would otherwise cost $15 for an adult. The second property necessary to understand this site is meal periods - there are 5 specific periods throughout per day, durations varying from two to four hours, where you can spend swipes. You cannot use swipes outside of meal periods. Additionally, if you try to use more than you cannot use more than 2 swipes in a single period...
