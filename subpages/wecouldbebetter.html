What are other schools doing?

Put simply, CSU is far better than some, somewhat worse than others. It's not the only school to implement something like meal periods - University of North Florida only allows one swipe per meal period, so we're at least not them. However, schools like University of Arizona don't have meal periods at all - and on top of that, resets swipes monthly rather than weekly. For a very thourough comparison, check the below spreadsheet!


The clear takeaway here should be that there's more than ample room for CSU to improve. We're falling behind compared to other schools, and that's not something I want to see. CSU rocks! So, its meal system should rock too.
